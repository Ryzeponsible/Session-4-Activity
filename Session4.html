Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken in buttermilk for at least 2 hours.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dredge each piece of chicken in the flour mixture.
  4. Heat the oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  5. Fry the chicken pieces until golden brown, about 10-15 minutes.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot. Enjoy!
